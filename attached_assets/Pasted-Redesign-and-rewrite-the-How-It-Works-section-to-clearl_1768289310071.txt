How It Works
1. Create Digital Campaigns Instantly

Business owners can create digital brochures within seconds instead of using traditional printed flyers.
By selecting campaign products from a wide product pool, users can instantly start building a digital promotion without design or technical knowledge.

2. Choose Products from a Rich Catalog

The application provides a large variety of product options that can be easily added to brochures.
Products can be updated dynamically, allowing campaigns to stay current and relevant at all times.

3. Flexible Brochure Builder

The brochure creation process is fully customizable:

Layouts

Product positioning

Text and visual hierarchy

Users can adjust every detail according to their campaign needs, making each brochure unique and brand-consistent.

4. Customizable Templates

Predefined templates are available and can be:

Modified

Rearranged

Reused

Templates help businesses create professional-looking brochures quickly while maintaining design consistency across campaigns.

5. Organized Management with Tenant Architecture

Thanks to the tenant-based architecture, companies can manage:

Multiple branches

Teams

Campaigns

All data remains structured, secure, and easy to control, even for large organizations.

6. Share on Social Media in Seconds

Once a brochure is ready, it can be shared instantly via social media integrations.
With a single action, campaigns can be published to platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp.

7. Promote and Advertise Your Brochures

A key advantage of e-Brochure is the ability to run social media advertisements directly from the created brochure.
Users can turn their brochures into ads and reach their target audience immediately.

8. Analyze Campaign Performance

The platform provides analytics and performance insights for promoted brochures, including:

Reach

Engagement

Interaction data

This allows businesses to measure campaign success and make data-driven decisions.

9. Modern, Fast, and Easy to Use

e-Brochure features a modern, intuitive interface designed for speed and simplicity.
The system is fast, responsive, and accessible from any device, enabling users to focus on campaigns rather than complexity.

Overall Goal

e-Brochure replaces traditional printed brochures with a dynamic, digital, and measurable marketing solution that helps businesses create, share, advertise, and analyze campaigns effortlessly.
